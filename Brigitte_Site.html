About Me

Hi everyone. I have decided to expand my educational horizons and try to learn computer code. I'm old but I'm smart. But I am also slow. But they say you are never too old to learn something new. So here I go. I am the eldest of two children of an Air Force family. Growing up, we traveled around a lot but I suppose now I call Chicago my home. My parents, brother and his family all live in Arizona now. I have one niece and two nephews. If you count my brother's two dogs, then I have four nephews.

I love to travel. I have lived abroad for two extensive stints and in total I have been to 28 different countries. The world is such an interesting place. If you don't believe me, get out and see for yourself. It's not as difficult or as expensive one may assume.

In college I studied anthropology. It is the study of human beings and their environment. With that in mind, I enjoy cultural events, diverse people and the excitement of living in a major metropolitan area. I consider Chicago the last great affordable city in America!

My Family

My Mom loves to play cards and is very sociable. Each visit is peppered with extensive games of bid whist and shopping. I have to mentally prepare myself for marathon sessions of both. My Dad is an introvert and a recent retiree. He spends most of his time on the computer, at the gym and watching professional sports. With the amount of time he is on the computer you'd think he knows how to code...Reuben, my brother, is five years younger than me and works in behavioral health services. He enjoys spending time with his family and making music. He also knows a little about coding. Talk about hidden talents...
